Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Create a free account in the app, and all your data will automatically sync across all your devices with the same account.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Open Settings app on your device → Tap your name → Subscriptions → Select Wealthly → Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions through the App Store app.

How do I delete my account?

Open Wealthly → Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Pro users can export all their data to CSV format from Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Data.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and never share your financial data with third parties. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.

What's included in Wealthly Pro?

Pro includes: cloud sync, ad-free experience, advanced reports, CSV import/export, priority support, and all future Pro features. See pricing in the app for details.

How do I get a refund?

All purchases are through Apple. Contact Apple Support or request a refund through reportaproblem.apple.com.

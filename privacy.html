Privacy Policy for Wealthly

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Introduction

Wealthly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Wealthly collects and stores the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and authentication
  • Financial Data: Transactions, budgets, categories, accounts, and net worth information that you manually enter
  • Portfolio Data: Stock holdings, investment data, and portfolio information you add
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app usage data
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Wealthly app services
  • Sync your financial data across your devices
  • Calculate and display your net worth, budgets, and portfolio performance
  • Send important account notifications and updates
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Third-Party Services

Wealthly uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage, authentication, and cloud synchronization
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to free users (Pro users see no ads)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies:

  • Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • We use secure authentication methods
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your personal and financial data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Correct: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-out: Decline optional data collection

Children's Privacy

Wealthly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
